# VidyaMitra вҖ“ AI Powered Learning & Career Assistant

VidyaMitra is a modern AI-powered web platform designed to enhance digital learning and career development. It supports students and professionals by providing personalized educational assistance, resume analysis, mock interviews, and skill recommendations.

The platform integrates Artificial Intelligence with a full-stack architecture to deliver smart recommendations, structured learning paths, and interactive user experiences.

## рҹ“ҡ Project Use Cases

### 1пёҸвғЈ Personalized Resume Evaluation & Skill Mapping
A final-year engineering student uploads their resume to VidyaMitra.  
The AI system analyzes the resume and identifies missing skills such as **data visualization or cloud fundamentals**, then recommends relevant courses to improve employability.

---

### 2пёҸвғЈ AI Mock Interview & Feedback
An MBA graduate practices interviews using VidyaMitraвҖҷs AI interview simulator.  
The system evaluates **tone, confidence, and accuracy**, providing feedback and suggestions for improvement.

---

### 3пёҸвғЈ Career Path Recommendation
A working professional looking to transition into **Data Science** uploads their resume.  
VidyaMitra identifies transferable skills and recommends a **structured learning roadmap with certifications**.
